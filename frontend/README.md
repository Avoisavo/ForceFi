# ForceFi Frontend

ForceFi is a decentralized prediction market platform running on the Linera blockchain. It allows users to bet on sports, casual events, and create their own markets.

## Getting Started

1.  **Connect Wallet**: Connect your Linera wallet to start trading.
2.  **Explore Markets**: Browse through the available sports and casual markets.
3.  **Place Bets**: Choose your outcome and place your bet with zero gas fees.
4.  **Create**: Have an idea for a market? Create it instantly!
